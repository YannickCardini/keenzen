Game Rules

Mercury — Old Dutch Board Game

Move all 4 of your marbles from their starting boxes, around the board, and into your home lane. First player to fill all 4 home slots wins.

🎲How to Play

  1. Each player receives 13 cards across 3 rounds (5 · 4 · 4).
  2. On your turn, play one card. You have 60 s — a move is auto-played when time runs out.
  3. Play A or K to enter a marble onto your start position.
  4. Landing on an opponent's marble sends it back to its starting box.
  5. Once past your start position, you enter the home lane — land exactly on a free slot.
  6. No legal move? Discard your hand and wait for the next deal.

🃏Card Actions

A Enter or move +1
K Enter a marble
Q Move forward +12
J Swap with any opponent
7 7 steps — split across 2 marbles
4 Move backward −4
2–10 Move one marble forward by face value

⚠️Key Rules

  • Freshly entered marble: A marble that has just entered the board on its start square (via an Ace or a King) is fully protected — it blocks passage, cannot be passed, captured, swapped, or moved backward. Once it moves forward, all protections are lifted, even if it later returns to the start square.
  • Jack restriction: Cannot swap with a marble already in a home lane.
