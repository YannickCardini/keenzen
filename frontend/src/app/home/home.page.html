Game Rules

Keezen — Old Dutch Board Game

🎯

Goal of the Game

Move all four of your marbles from your starting boxes, around the board, and into your home lane. First player to get all 4 marbles home wins.

👥

Players

4 players, every man for himself. First to get all 4 marbles into their home lane wins.

🎲

How to Play

  • Each player gets 13 cards dealt over 3 rounds (5 · 4 · 4). Play proceeds clockwise.
  • On your turn, play one card and perform its action. You have 30 seconds — time runs out, a move is played automatically.
  • Entering: Play an A or K to place a marble on your start position.
  • Capturing: Landing exactly on another marble sends it back to its starting box.
  • Home lane: When passing your own start position, you enter the home lane. You must land exactly on a free slot — no overshooting.
  • No legal move? Discard your entire hand and wait for the next deal.
🃏

Important Rules

🛡️
Safe zones: A marble on its own start position is invincible — it blocks passage and cannot be captured or swapped. Home lane marbles are also safe.
🏠
Home lane order: Marbles fill the home lane from the back slot forward. You cannot jump over your own marbles inside the lane.
🔄
Jack restriction: Cannot swap with a marble that is on its own start position or already in a home lane.
